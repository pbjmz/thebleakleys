What This Module Covers

This instructional module is designed for undergraduate students seeking to strengthen their study habits through proven methods in metacognition, organization, and time management.

Learning Goals

  • Evaluate current study behaviors and identify areas for improvement
  • Apply metacognitive strategies for learning
  • Implement note-taking and scheduling techniques
  • Practice active learning methods
  • Develop a personalized and sustainable study plan
